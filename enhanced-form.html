Football Glossary

Goalkeeper
The player who guards the goal and is the only one allowed to use their hands within the penalty area.
Offside
A rule violation that occurs when an attacking player is ahead of the last defender when the ball is played to them.
Hat-trick
When a player scores three goals in a single match.
Penalty Kick
A free shot at goal from the penalty spot, awarded after a foul in the penalty area.
Corner Kick
A kick from the corner of the field awarded when the ball crosses the goal line last touched by a defender.
